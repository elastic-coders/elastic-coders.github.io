Methodology

Get your wheel spinning.

  • Getting to know your needs

    SPRINT 0

    We analyse the project objectives and define the relevant user stories. We than plan our work together with you in order to fulfill all demands.

  • Kicking off the development

    SPRINT 2-3

    We develop the product focusing on the minimum time to market possible and iterating in the most flexible way according to the changes in market opportunities and project guidelines.

  • Building user stories

    SPRINT 4-8

    During the initial iterations on your product, we always focus on aspects from the user's poit of view, making our progress precise and testable right away as you go along with the validation.

    Once ready, we help you through the launch and we help you understand and monitor the results. We provide maintainance and improvements right away, in line with the initial feedbacks coming from your users.
