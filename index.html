The Elastic Coders worked and explored the sofware industry for many years and realized how technology ought to be used to empower new visions:

This is what out clients want and what we do our best to provide.

How we craft your product

A handfull of skilled and expert senior developers will take hold of your vision and help you structure it. User stories, technical analysis and development are performed together with you to fullfill all specific demands.

Our technology becomes your technology. Because of that, it is carefully put together with a special care to manutenability and scaling capabilities. It is chosen considering adoption, performance, ease of use and ecosystem. Open Source, dynamic languages and extensive framework usage are the basis of all projects.

The stack idesigned gorund-up to be fast, scalable and long-lasting; we want our efforts to run efficiently and be easy to maintain over the years. We keep it lean and sensible.

Check out our opensource contributions to get a feeling of what we like most.

How we build your team

Very soon your new product will be ready for prime time and you’ll need an iternal team to maintain and to enhance it. Right from the beginning of the project we help you with job interviews, later on we inlcude your internal resources into our development process and finally your team will be able to take over.

The most productive and yielding development process is affined and passed over during the joint development phase. We embrace agile methods: Scrum makes us and our partner teams highly productive.
