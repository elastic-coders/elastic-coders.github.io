What are cookies ?
Cookies are small text files that the sites visited by the user sends to your browser ( Internet Explorer , Mozilla Firefox , Google Chrome , Opera , etc . ) , Where they are stored before being re-transmitted to the same sites at the next visit to the same user .
During the navigation of a site, you can get on to their terminals also cookie sites of different web server ( cd cookie "third parties " ) ; this is because the website visited can be present elements as , for example , images , maps , sounds , social buttons ( "Like" Facebook , " +1 " to google , etc . ) specific links to web pages of other domains residing on a server other than the one where you find the page required . In other words , these are cookies that are set by a website other than the one you are currently visiting .

 

Cookies are defined technical ones, used for the sole purpose of "carrying out the transmission of a communication over an electronic communications network, or as strictly necessary for the provider of an information society service explicitly requested by the subscriber or user to provide this service "(cfr. art. 122, paragraph 1, of the Code).
Cookies profiling, however, are used to create profiles user and are used in order to send advertising messages in line with the preferences shown by the same part of surfing the net. They refers to the art. 122 of the Code where it provides that "the storage of information in the terminal equipment of a contractor or a user or access to information already stored, are only allowed on condition that the contractor or the user has given consent after It was informed by the simplified procedures referred to in Article 13, paragraph 3 "(art. 122, paragraph 1, of the Code).
